Products Overview

Maintailing world-class quality has been a corner stone in SK Group's business strategy. The company has invested extensively in manufacturing facilities to maintain the premium quality standards. Three state-of-the- art manufacturing units collectively produce over Rs. 280 million of intonational quality products of auto, white goods telecom components annually, giving it a clear edge in the market place. The Group is a recognized manufacturer and poscessor of different grades of foams and molded plastic fastener products that are used for Insulation, Heat Resistance, Shock Resistance, Filtration, Packing, Gaskets, Seals, Sound Absorbers and EXternal Packaging for dedicated goods by Automobile, Telecom and White Goods Manufactures.
